Welcome to my portfolio website.

I am currently pursuing an bachelor degree in Art History with a minor in Psychology at Pace University.

Presently, I am an administrative intern at Onassis ONX. Since working there I have helped coordinate the 2024 Summer Showcase, the 2024 Games for Change Immersive Arcade, as well as Goup Hug

My gallery experience includes curating "We're Home" at the Pace University gallery through the Amelia A. Gould research assistantship. Additionally, I have experience in art handeling and installation, notably aiding for the instalations of Firoz Mahmud: Inscaping Legacies, Mie Yim: Mother Octopus, and Journey Illustrated.

Beyong my love of art and my intention to work in the art world, I have other interests such as baking, raising my dog, traveling, and European football.
